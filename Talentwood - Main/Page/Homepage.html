How it Works

Submit your short film

Film directors can submit their short films through films through Talentwood app, along with a brief description and cast/crew details

Get rated by the audience

The short films will be uploaded on both Talentwood app and YouTube for viewers to watch and rate. the top 10 films with the highest ratings will be selected

Create a feature film

The top 10 directors will then have the opportunity to create a full- length feature film based on their short film. These movies will be judged by industry experts and the winner will receive a big prize form Talentwood
