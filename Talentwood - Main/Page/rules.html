Submission Guideline

1
Eligibility
  • You are a emerging director with a passion for storytelling.
  • Your film is a short film, typically running under 10 - 15 minutes.
  • Your film is an original work, and you have obtained all the necessary
    rights and permissions for its submission.
2
Submission
  • Visit the 'Submit Film' page on our website
  • Fill out the required information, including your film's title, genre,
    director's name, and a brief synopsis
  • Upload your film in the specified format. (Details on the preferred
    file format and size can be found on the submission page.)
3
Review and Selection
  • Once your submission is in, our panel of judges will carefully review each entry.
  • We'll be looking for creativity, storytelling, and technical excellence.
    We'll keep you updated on the status of your submission via email.
  • Be patient as we go through all the entries; you'll be the first
    to know if your film has made the cut
4
Top 10 Selection
  • This is where things get exciting! The top 10 films will be determined
    by the number of views and likes your film receives on our YouTube platform.
  • Your fans and supporters can make all the difference, so share
    your film far and wide to increase your chances.
  • Stay tuned for the announcement of the top 10 finalists
    on our website and social media channels
5
Director's Challenge
  • If your film makes it to the top 10, you'll face the Director's Challenge.
  • Each finalist will create one more short film based on a specific theme.
  • The ultimate winner of this challenge will secure a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity
    to direct a feature film under our production.
Submission Guideline

Originality Matters:

We celebrate originality and creativity. All submissions must be entirely your own work. Plagiarism or the use of copyrighted material without permission is not allowed.

Film Length:

To keep the competition engaging and accessible, short films should not exceed 20 minutes in length. Concise storytelling often leaves a lasting impact.

Genere Freedom:

We welcome short films of all genres – drama, comedy, animation, documentary, and more. Let your imagination run wild and choose the genre that best fits your vision.

Rights and Permissions:

By submitting your film, you grant us the right to showcase it on our platform and during events. Ensure you have all necessary permissions for any copyrighted material used in your film.
